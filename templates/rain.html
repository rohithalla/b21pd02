Rainfall forecasting

Where?

The rainfall is forecasted for a period of one month at three different regions namely Coastal Andhra Pradesh, Telangana and Rayalaseema. 

Purpose

The next one month data i.e 30 days data will be forecasted. Farmers can accordingly plan their water resources and its usage. 
Note: If the rainfall data at particular area is available then we can further increase the study to classify how intensively rainfall is going to occur.
